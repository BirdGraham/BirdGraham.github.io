1. Which of the following represents the standard form equation of a circle centered at (3, -4) with a radius of 5?

Explanation: The standard equation of a circle is (x - h)2 + (y - k)2 = r2. Substituting h = 3, k = -4, and r = 5 yields (x - 3)2 + (y + 4)2 = 25.

2. Solve the following equation for x: 3x - 7 = 5x + 9

Explanation: Subtract 3x from both sides to get -7 = 2x + 9. Subtract 9 from both sides to get -16 = 2x. Dividing by 2 yields x = -8.

3. Find the slope of the line passing through the points (2, 5) and (4, 11).

Explanation: Use the slope formula m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1). Thus, m = (11 - 5) / (4 - 2) = 6 / 2 = 3.

4. What are the solutions to the quadratic equation x2 - 9 = 0?

Explanation: Factoring the difference of squares gives (x - 3)(x + 3) = 0, which yields x = 3 and x = -3.

5. Simplify the expression: (2x3y2)(4xy5)

Explanation: Multiply coefficients (2 * 4 = 8) and add the corresponding exponents for base x (3 + 1 = 4) and base y (2 + 5 = 7).

6. If f(x) = 2x2 - 3x + 5, determine the value of f(-2).

Explanation: Evaluate f(-2) = 2(-2)2 - 3(-2) + 5 = 2(4) + 6 + 5 = 8 + 6 + 5 = 19.

7. What is the value of x in the right triangle if the hypotenuse is 10 and one leg is 6?

Explanation: Using the Pythagorean theorem: a2 + b2 = c2 -> 62 + x2 = 102 -> 36 + x2 = 100 -> x2 = 64 -> x = 8.

8. If log2(x) = 5, what is the value of x?

Explanation: Convert the logarithmic statement into an exponential equation: x = 25. Evaluating 25 yields 32.

9. A bag contains 3 red marbles, 5 blue marbles, and 2 green marbles. What is the probability of choosing a red marble?

Explanation: Total number of marbles is 3 + 5 + 2 = 10. The number of red marbles is 3, making the probability 3/10.

10. What is the sum of the interior angles of a convex hexagon (6 sides)?

Explanation: Use the interior angle sum formula: (n - 2) * 180°. Substituting n = 6 gives (6 - 2) * 180° = 4 * 180° = 720°.

4. Choose the best replacement for the underlined word: The corporate policy was written to be intentional, clear, and verbose so that nobody would misunderstand it.

5. Choose the correct verb form: Neither the manager nor the employees ______ present at the safety meeting yesterday morning.
