Question 1
Information and Ideas / Command of Evidence
In many high-altitude ecosystems, soil nutrient availability acts as a strict constraint on plant biomass. Ecologist Dr. Elena Vance hypothesized that specific subalpine cushion plants actively alter their immediate substrate chemistry, creating localized microenvironments that facilitate the survival of neighboring, less-hardy flora.
Which finding, if true, would most directly support Dr. Vance’s hypothesis?
Correct Answer: B. Dr. Vance's hypothesis hinges entirely on cushion plants actively changing their substrate chemistry to help nearby plants. Showing that soil directly underneath these plants contains higher bioavailable nitrogen (a nutrient) explicitly confirms this chemical modification.
Question 2
Craft and Structure / Words in Context
The corporate governance committee insisted that the executive's record, far from being completely unblemished, was actually marked by strategic omissions. Yet, because those omissions never technically violated local statutes, the final compliance report described his tenure as entirely _______.
Which choice completes the text with the most logical and precise word?
Correct Answer: A. The sentence establishes a contrast using "Yet." Even though there were omissions, they "never technically violated local statutes." Therefore, the report must describe his tenure as legal or permitted by law. "Licit" directly means lawful or legal.
Question 3
Expression of Ideas / Transitions
Art historian Clara Simmons argues that mid-twentieth-century muralists were driven primarily by public accessibility, placing large-scale works in subways and public squares. _______, contemporary street artists often utilize identical urban surfaces but are frequently motivated by a desire to subvert commercial ownership of public visual spaces.
Which choice completes the text with the most logical transition?
Correct Answer: D. The passage compares two eras of artists using the same spaces but for different core reasons: mid-century muralists wanted "public accessibility," while contemporary street artists want to "subvert commercial ownership." This is a clear contrast, making "By contrast" the correct fit.
Question 4
Advanced Math / Nonlinear Functions
A bacterial population triples in size every 4 hours. If the initial population size at t = 0 is 150, which of the following expressions represents the total population size after t hours?
Correct Answer: C. Exponential growth is modeled by the format P = I(R)^(t/p), where I is the initial value (150), R is the rate multiplier (3), and p is the interval period (4 hours). Therefore, the correct exponent tracking the total hours step-by-step is t/4.
Question 5
Algebra / Linear Equations
A local catering company charges a flat setup fee of 85 dollars plus an additional 24 dollars per guest. If a client's total bill before tax was 1165 dollars, how many guests attended the event?
Correct Answer: B. Set up the linear equation: 24g + 85 = 1165. Subtracting 85 from both sides gives 24g = 1080. Dividing both sides by 24 gives g = 45 guests.
